# AI & Predictive Analytics Demo Guide
**Capstone Defense: Government Service Transport & Mobility Management**

This guide explains how to demonstrate the Predictive Analytics feature of your dashboard.

## 1. The Core Concept
Your system solves the "Over/Under Deployment" problem by predicting passenger demand.
-   **System Data (Tickets/Payments):** Good for financial tracking, but often incomplete for crowd size (e.g., people waiting but not yet paid).
-   **Manual Observations (The "Ground Truth"):** The AI needs to know the *actual* number of people at the terminal to be accurate.
-   **The Solution:** You combine both. The "Data Inputs" form allows dispatchers to log the real crowd size, which "trains" the AI to be more than 80% accurate.

## 3. Technical Explanation (For Q&A)
If they ask "How does it work?":
-   **Algorithm:** It uses a Time-Series Weighted Average.
    -   `Forecast = (Average of last 4 same-weekdays) * (Recent Trend Factor)`
    -   *Example:* To predict next Monday 8 AM, it looks at the last 4 Mondays at 8 AM.
-   **External Factors:**
    -   **Weather:** Fetched via API. Heavy rain reduces predicted demand by 20% (configurable).
    -   **Events:** Holidays (fetched via Nager.Date API) treat the day like a Sunday (lower commuter demand).
    -   **Traffic & Incidents:** TomTom traffic flow + incident feed helps explain delays that affect terminal turnaround and route headways.

-   **"Accuracy is low."** -> Log more "Data Inputs". The more "Ground Truth" you provide, the better it gets.
